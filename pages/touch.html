1) A Legacy of Civilizations — Hoi An Ancient Town

Departure
9:00 AM or 3:00 PM
Duration
2.5–3 hours
Transportation
Walking & E-Car
Pick-up
Directly from your hotel

Overview

Step into the timeless charm of Hoi An, a UNESCO town where East meets West. This guided city walk traces the golden era of the 17th-century trading port and the legacies it left on architecture, education, religion, and daily life. Discover the city’s rise and decline, and how locals protect their identity even after international recognition — more than a walk, it’s a journey through heritage, transformation, and living tradition.

Highlights & Stops

  • Confucius Temple – Moral pillars and educational foundations in Hoi An.
  • Japanese Covered Bridge – Icon of hospitality and cultural fusion.
  • Hoi An Market – Lively trading rhythms, flavors, and traditions.
  • Hoi An Catholic Church – Early Western influence and the birth of Romanized Vietnamese script.

Notes

  • Free guiding: Our guiding service is free of charge.
  • Personal expenses: Guests cover entrance tickets, food/drinks, and any transport costs (if used) for both you and our guides.

City walk with optional E-Car segments for comfort. Wear comfortable shoes, bring sun/rain protection, and keep hydrated. Hotel pick-up included.

2) Path to Deity — My Son Sanctuary

Departure
8:00 AM or 2:00 PM
Duration
3.5–4 hours
Transportation
Motorbike or Car
Pick-up
Directly from your hotel

Overview

Journey into the spiritual heart of the Champa civilization. Nestled in a lush valley, My Son tells a story of faith, power, and resilience along the Maritime Silk Road. Among temple ruins and sacred towers, uncover an ancient culture whose traces still shape Central Vietnam’s identity today.

Highlights & Stops

  • My Son Sanctuary – Religious heart of Champa; temple towers, art, and ongoing preservation.
  • Tra Kieu Holy Mother Center – Symbol of cultural and religious harmony with matriarchal traditions.
  • Urban Axis: My Son – Tra Kieu – Hoi An – Follow the sacred Thu Bon River linking religion, administration, and economy.

Notes

  • Free guiding: Our guiding service is free of charge.
  • Personal expenses: Guests cover entrance tickets to My Son, food/drinks, and transport costs if applicable for both you and our guides.

Expect sun and uneven terrain — wear comfortable clothes and closed shoes. Motorbike or car are available on request; we’ll suggest the best option based on your group size and weather.
