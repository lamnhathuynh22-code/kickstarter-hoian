Kickstarter Hoian – Youth Empowerment

Nurturing Local Youth for Sustainable Tourism

Welcome to Kickstarter Hoian — where future tour guides take their first big step. We’re a nonprofit training club that helps students and gap-year adventurers turn their love for travel into real guiding experience.

Our young guides are cultural ambassadors, connecting Vietnam with the world one smile at a time. Based in the heart of Central Vietnam, we mix learning with laughter, history with adventure, and locals with curious travelers.

Every tour is a two-way exchange — guests discover hidden gems, and our trainees discover their voice. Along the way, we’re keeping local traditions alive and giving the next generation a reason to be proud of their roots.

At Kickstarter Hoian, journeys begin – and cultures connect.
