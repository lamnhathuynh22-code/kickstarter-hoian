Art Hoian

Discover the authentic soul of Hoi An through its living arts — where every village, artisan, and daily rhythm tells a story of creativity.

1) A Living Museum of Craftsmanship — Cycling to Kim Bồng & Trà Nhiêu

Departure
8:00 AM
Duration
3.5–4 hours
Transportation
Bicycle (motorbike/car on request)
Pick-up
Kickstarter bicycle garage or your hotel

Overview

Ride beyond Hoi An’s Ancient Town and step into a living museum of traditional crafts. This cycling journey takes you through rice fields and bamboo-lined village paths to Kim Bồng and Trà Nhiêu, where generations of artisans quietly keep their heritage alive. Along the way, you’ll meet kind-hearted locals, feel the rhythm of rural life, and witness a world where culture is lived every day — authentic, humble, and full of life.

Tour Highlights & Stops

  • Cycle through scenic rice fields and quiet lanes.
  • Explore Kim Bồng & Trà Nhiêu craft villages.
  • Meet welcoming local families.
  • Peaceful boat ride on the Thu Bon River.

Notes

  • Free guiding: Our guiding service is free of charge.
  • Personal expenses: Guests only cover entrance fees, meals, drinks, and any transportation if needed for both you and our guides.

These routes are best explored by bicycle. If you already have a bike, that’s perfectly fine — or you can rent our mountain bike for 70.000 VND per tour for extra comfort and safety. Wear comfortable clothes, closed shoes, and bring sun protection. Motorbike or car can be arranged upon request.

2) The Art of Everyday Life — Cycling to Thanh Hà & Trà Quế

Departure
8:00 AM
Duration
3.5–4 hours
Transportation
Bicycle (motorbike/car on request)
Pick-up
Kickstarter bicycle garage or your hotel

Overview

Pottery, herbs & buffalo — all in one ride! Leave the bustling center behind and cycle through peaceful countryside to Thanh Hà Pottery Village and the organic gardens of Trà Quế. Then hop on a friendly buffalo for a short, fun ride across rice fields. Short but full of life, this journey connects you with the land, the people, and the joyful spirit that makes Hoi An truly unique.

Tour Highlights & Stops

  • Cycle through rice paddies and quiet village paths.
  • Visit Thanh Hà Pottery Village — watch artisans at work and try shaping your own clay piece.
  • Explore Trà Quế Organic Village and join hands-on farming activities.
  • Enjoy a fun buffalo ride across green rice fields.
  • Return to the Ancient Town with local lunch recommendations from your guide.

Notes

  • Free guiding: Our guiding service is free of charge.
  • Personal expenses: Guests only cover entrance fees, meals, drinks, and any transportation if needed.

These routes are ideal for bicycles. You can use your own bike or rent our mountain bike — 70.000 VND per tour for better comfort. Wear comfortable clothes and bring sun protection. Motorbike or car can be arranged upon request.
